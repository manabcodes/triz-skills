# Altshuller Contradiction Matrix — Lookup Table

Full 39×39 matrix. Find your **improving parameter** (the row), then your **worsening parameter** (the column letter/number) to get the recommended Inventive Principle numbers. Look up principle names/descriptions in `inventive_principles.md`.

Cells with no entry mean no statistically dominant principle was identified by Altshuller for that pairing — in that case, consider all 40 principles, or reformulate the contradiction with different parameters.

**The matrix is asymmetric**: row A × column B usually gives different principles than row B × column A.
